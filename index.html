Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

This website is operated by Aidan Wheaton trading as Dunvale Studio, a web design studio based in Staffordshire, England. You can contact us at aidan@dunvalestudio.co.uk.

What data we collect

When you fill in the RSVP form on this website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address (if provided)
  • Your attendance status
  • Your meal preference and dietary requirements
  • Any message you choose to include

We do not collect any payment information. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels of any kind.

Why we collect it

The information collected through the RSVP form is used solely to plan the wedding — specifically to manage guest attendance numbers, meal requirements, and dietary needs. It is not used for any other purpose and will not be shared with any third party beyond the couple and their immediate wedding planning.

How your data is stored

When you submit the RSVP form, your details are sent directly to the wedding couple via a secure form service (Formspree). Your response is emailed to Beth and Damian immediately. No personal RSVP data is stored in your browser. The public guest list displayed on the RSVP page shows only your name and attendance status for the duration of your visit — this information is not saved once you close the page.

How long we keep your data

RSVP data is held until after the wedding has taken place, after which it will be cleared. If you would like your response removed before then, please contact us at aidan@dunvalestudio.co.uk.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics of any kind.

The website is served through Cloudflare and hosted on Vercel, both of which may set strictly necessary functional cookies to ensure security and performance. These cannot be disabled as they are required for the website to function correctly.

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please email aidan@dunvalestudio.co.uk and we will respond within 30 days.

If you believe your data has not been handled correctly you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Contact

For any privacy questions: aidan@dunvalestudio.co.uk
